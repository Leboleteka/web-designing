BUSINESS DESCRIPTION

History of the business

Space Butcher Shop was founded in 2017 by Lebohang Leteka. It is located in Leribe Hlotse near SMS suppermarket near Metlakhola building

Our Mission

Our Missionis to provide the highest quality meat products to our customers, while supporting local farmers and sustainable practices. we aim to build strong brand and relationships with our customers also to be positive force to the community.

Our Vision

we want to be known as a trusted source for high quality meat and we want to be a valued member of the community. we aim to be a leader in the local food movement, and we want to set the standart for sustanability and ethical practices. we believe that our commitment to quality and sustantability will lead to a brighter future for our customers and for our community to create a place where customers could get fresh, delicious meat that sourced from local Basotho farmers.

My Objective

I started this business after realising that there was a need for high quality butcher shop in Hlotse. I wanted to create a place where customers could get fresh and delicious meat sourced fron local farmers. You will agree with me that meat sourced from other countries is not healthy, it causes sickness like cancer because their intention is to make money not health bases. We eat packaged meat which contain chemicals which prevent it to lose color and taste, eventouh it was packed years ago. I have a passion to serve healthy variety of fresh meat to the community, I wanted to create a business that reflected those values

Target Market

our target market is non vegetarian with all ages individually or local restaurants in Leribe. People who earn low can affort to buy due to low and affortable prices.
